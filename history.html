History of Ceylon Train

Ceylon Train is first stablished in 1864 with the main purpose of transportation of coffee and other agricultural plantations. The first railway line build from kandy to colombo port. After that main line extended to anuradhapura, Kankesanturai and Batticaloa. similer branch line laid to nanu oya to collect tea harvest around nuwara eliya.

Now the ceylon railway network is about 1500 KM long and these railway tracks pases most beautifull and scenic routes including waterfalls, mountains, tea states, forests and bridges.
